OUTFITS

When it comes to outfits, everything is very flexible!

I just need to know your preferences and the styles you like, and we’ll start with your favorites.

During the photoshoot, we can change ideas and try different looks.

Since I don’t know you personally yet, it’s a bit difficult for me to suggest something specific, but we can work together to choose the best options.

We’ll begin with your favorite outfits, and step by step, we’ll decide on the rest as we go.

When planning your outfits for a maternity photoshoot, consider my special option—romantic flying fabrics.

They bring endless creativity and make every photo truly unique, as I shape each wave with my artistic vision.

No two images will ever be the same!
