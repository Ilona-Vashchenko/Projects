Hi, I’m Veronika, a maternity and pregnancy photographer based in London.

What once was just a dream has become my career—I still remember the excitement of unboxing my first camera and exploring the world of photography.

Today, I feel lucky to capture the beauty of motherhood and create timeless memories.

Having started my photography journey just a few years ago, I’m grateful to have received the Outstanding Maternity Award 2024 twice—Seasons 33 and 34—recognizing me as the best maternity photographer in the UK.
