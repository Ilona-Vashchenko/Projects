Welcome to The Wynd Cafe & Bistro!

The Wynd House cafe & Bistro Is a modern Independent Cafe along Post House Wynd in the heart of Darlington town centre.

Our menu consists of freshly cooked breakfasts, a cafe style lunch serving a variety of hot and cold dishes.
We also have a Ukrainian menu consisting of delicious dishes. At our cafe we offer a warm, welcoming environment for all our customers whether you are looking to enjoy a great local coffee blend, cold drinks, alcohol drinks, cocktails as well as our popular signature milkshakes.

We now offer Dinner meal, Charcoal Grill Menu with a variety of Cocktails and other alcohol drinks!

Our goal is to provide you with that great cafe experience and a relaxing atmosphere.
